About ISUZU MUX

The mu-X now gets an extended warranty for its BS4 powertrain for upto 8-years/2,00,000km. Engines: It is available in two variants - 4x2 and 4x4. Under the hood is a 3.0-litre, four-cylinder turbo-diesel engine producing 177PS of power and 380Nm of torque. Transmission duties will be handled by a 5-speed automatic transmission with sequential shift and brake shift technology. The claimed fuel-efficiency for the 4x2 variant is 13.8kmpl. In terms of features, the Isuzu MUX houses an electrically-adjustable driver’s seat, second row seats with one-touch fold and tumble, a power outlet each for all three rows, automatic climate control, cooling vents for all three rows, a roof-mounted 10-inch DVD monitor, cruise control and a 7.0-inch touchscreen infotainment system with 8-speakers and iPod and Bluetooth compatibility. It is the successor of the dated MU-7 (now discontinued) and is more competitive in its segment as well compared to the latter. The Isuzu MU-X undercuts two of its rivals, the Ford Endeavour and the Toyota Fortuner in terms of price.



ISUZU MUX OverView

If you want a burly and spacious full-size SUV, and the most affordable one at that, look no further than the Isuzu MU-X. It offers an exemplary ride quality as well. However, it lacks finesse, both in terms of design and refinement, which the competition offers. It is also not the most feature-rich SUV in its segment.
