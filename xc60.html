About Volvo XC60

Volvo has launched a new base-spec Momentum variant of the XC60. It is Rs 7 lakh cheaper than the top-spec Inscription variant and is priced at Rs 52.9 lakh (ex-showroom pan-India). Read more on this here. Volvo XC60 is available in India in two variants- Momentum and Inscription, which are priced at Rs 52.9 lakh and Rs 59.9 lakh (ex-showroom pan-India) respectively. The new XC60 is based on the same, Scalable Product Architecture (SPA) platform as the XC90 and also borrows design elements and a lot of features from its elder sibling. It features new LED headlamps and redesigned 'Thor's Hammer' LED DRLs that extend up to the grille. On the rear end, it has the Volvo's signature L-shaped tail-light cluster with LED lights. Step inside and you'll see a 12.3-inch touchscreen infotainment system on the centre console. It also features a four-zone climate control system and has aircon controls for the rear passengers too. Initially, it'll be imported as CBUs and will be later assembled at its Bengaluru facility. It is expected to be priced around the Rs 60 lakh mark and will compete with the likes of the Audi Q5, BMW X3, Mercedes-Benz GLC and the Lexus NX.



Volvo XC60 OverView

The all-new Volvo XC60 is a huge improvement over its predecessor, especially in terms of overall space, comfort, and features. Plus, it has all the things you love Volvo for - tough build, ease of driving, safety features, easy to live with, and the styling is nothing short of eye-catching too. Although the overall emphasis certainly tilts towards luxury, the sprightly motor makes driving a breeze.
