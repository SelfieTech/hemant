About Isuzu D-Max V-Cross

Isuzu has introduced a limited edition of the V-Cross with an automatic transmission. Isuzu D-Max V-Cross Variants and Prices: The V-Cross is available in three variants now: Standard and High, which are priced at Rs 15.51 lakh and 17.03 lakh (ex-showroom Mumbai), respectively, along with the newly launched limited edition named the Z-Prestige. It is priced at Rs 19.99 lakh (ex-showroom Delhi). Isuzu D-Max V-Cross Engine: The V-Cross is powered by a 2.5-litre diesel engine that makes 134PS of power and 320Nm of peak torque while mated to a 5-speed manual gearbox whereas the Z-Prestige is offered with a 1.9-litre diesel engine which puts out 150PS of power and 350Nm of torque. What’s more, you also get a first-in-segment 6-speed automatic transmission option in the limited edition version. Isuzu D-Max V-Cross Features: The V-Cross is equipped with dual front airbags, ABS with EBD and brake assist as well as ISOFIX child seat anchors as standard. It also gets traction control and electronic stability control, but they are limited to the High variant. Other features on offer include projector headlamps with LED DRLs, cruise control, six-way electrically adjustable driver seat, auto climate control, electrically adjustable and foldable ORVMs and reverse parking camera. The Z-prestige variant flaunts a two-tone Brown-Grey combination perforated leather seats, live-surround speakers on the roof, six airbags and a Brake-Override System (BOS).The dual-cockpit design is further enhanced by the new instrument cluster housing a new 3D Design Electroluminescent Meter and Gear Shift Indicators. Isuzu D-Max V-Cross Rivals: The V-Cross doesn’t have any natural rivals in the market.



Isuzu D-Max V-Cross OverView

The V-Cross is the only adventure utility vehicle available in India or at least that's how Isuzu wants to market it. Reasonably so, as lifestyle pick-up trucks still don't have a demand back here. However, this V-Cross has the right potential to create this demand with its mean road presence, go-anywhere capability while providing many utilitarian benefits.
