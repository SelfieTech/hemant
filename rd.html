About Honda Accord

Honda Accord Price: Honda Accord price starts at ₹ 44.28 Lakh. The price of Hybrid (Electric + Petrol) variant for Accord is ₹ 44.28 Lakh. Honda Accord Versions: Accord is available in 1 variants. All the variants are Automatic (CVT). Honda Accord Colours: Accord is offered in 4 colours: White Orchid Pearl, Crystal Black Pearl, Modern Steel Metallic and Lunar Silver Metallic. However, some of these colours are available in specific versions. Honda Accord Competitors: Accord is competing against Toyota Camry, BMW 3 Series, BMW 3 Series GT, Mercedes-Benz C-Class, Skoda Octavia, Skoda Superb, Jaguar XE, Jaguar XF and Lexus ES.
