About BMW 2-Series

Latest Update: BMW has introduced a limited-run edition of the 2 Series Gran Coupe sedan. Check it out here. BMW 2 Series Gran Coupe Price: The entry-level BMW sedan is priced from Rs 39.30 lakh to Rs 41.40 lakh while the limited edition model is priced at Rs 42.30 lakh (ex-showroom). BMW 2 Series Gran Coupe Variants: It is available in two variants: 220d Sport Line and 220d M Sport. BMW 2 Series Gran Coupe Powertrain: For now, BMW offers the 2 Series Gran Coupé only with a 2.0-litre turbocharged diesel engine churning out 190PS and 400Nm. It drives the front wheels via an 8-speed automatic transmission and helps the car go from 0-100kmph in 7.5 seconds. BMW 2 Series Gran Coupe Features: BMW offers the sedan with a 12.3-inch digital instrument cluster, a 10.25-inch touchscreen infotainment system with Apple CarPlay, ambient lighting, panoramic sunroof, and wireless charging. BMW 2 Series Gran Coupe Safety: Safety features on offer include six airbags, electronic differential lock control, ABS with brake assist, and dynamic stability control. BMW 2 Series Gran Coupe Rivals: The BMW sedan will rival the upcoming Mercedes-Benz A-Class sedan and Audi A3.



BMW 2-Series OverView

The 220d M Sport gets a new limited Black Shadow Edition featuring blacked-out trim and M Performance parts. The exterior gets a glossy black mesh grille and an M Performance spoiler as well as 18-inch black alloy wheels. The interior follows the same theme with sporty black upholstery. The special edition is based on the top-spec 220d M Sport variant and utilises its 190PS 2.0-litre diesel engine. The Black Shadow Edition is limited to just 24 units. It’s priced at Rs 42.30 lakh (ex-showroom), a premium of Rs 90,000 over the M Sport variant it’s based on.
